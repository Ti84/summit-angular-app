About our Organization

The North American Summit was started in 2001. What began as a trip to Vegas, turned into a yearly gathering. There are four men that were present in 2001 that continue to keep the organization's goal intact. John Arnold, Stuart Ponting, Norman Suvan, and Casey Wood. While Stuart and John will argue that the idea of a yearly Summit was their idea, Norm and Casey have spent more time and dedication to keep the Summit alive The Summit began in the United States and every year the venue changes between Canada and the US. Check out the Previous Summits page to see pictures and read past peace talks. Our current goal is to turn this Summit into a non-profit organization that will benefit charities in both the United States and Canada.
